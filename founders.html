The Founders

SUN & MOON

Sisters, artists and creative directors, Sun and Moon founded STARGIRLS as a world of music, image and atmosphere.

Sun and Moon, New York, 2026

Sun and Moon did not begin with a company. They began with songs, photographs, unfinished videos, borrowed rooms, changing cities and the instinct to make something of their own.

Their work is shaped by the details around them: a white dress against darkness, silver jewelry, black hair, handwritten notes, late-night recording sessions, the ritual of getting ready, the image before the performance.

STARGIRLS became the name for that language.

BEGINNINGS

Music entered their lives before it became a profession. Rehearsals, performances, recording, cameras and movement between homes formed the background of their childhood.

What began as participation became discipline. They learned to write, record, edit, direct and build their own visual world before waiting for one to be given to them.

THE CODES

Their codes are recurring: black and white, silver and gold, skin and shadow, softness and severity, the stage and the private room.

These contrasts appear in the music, the films, the imagery and the first fragrances. Each detail is treated as part of the same language.

SUN

Sun is a vocalist, performer and image-maker. Her presence is cinematic, intimate and direct. She is drawn to romance, memory, movement and the emotional weight of a scene.

In the world of STARGIRLS, Sun brings warmth, voice, performance and light. Her image is soft but never passive: luminous skin, long hair, quiet glamour, and a sense of feeling held just below the surface.

MOON

Moon is an artist, editor and creative director. She is drawn to structure, visual identity, sound, packaging and the architecture of a world.

Her work is precise and restrained. She studies how a title looks, how a page breathes, how a song sits beside an image, and why an object deserves to exist.

STARGIRLS

STARGIRLS is the meeting point of their disciplines: music, fragrance, film, image and objects. It is not a separation of projects, but a single world expressed through different forms.

The first era, ANEW, introduced the sound. The first fragrances, SUN and MOON, begin the house of scent.

IN THEIR WORDS

“Beauty is everlasting.”
