About

THE FOUNDERS

Sun and Moon, sisters, artists and creative directors.

Sun and Moon, New York, 2026

Sun and Moon founded STARGIRLS as an extension of their own world: music first, then image, fragrance, beauty and objects. Before it became a name, it was a way of making things together.

Their story began between changing homes, studios, photographs, rehearsals and unfinished songs. They learned to build with what they had, to direct their own image, and to treat every detail as part of the work.

SUN

Sun is a vocalist, performer and image-maker. Her creative language is intimate, cinematic and immediate. She is drawn to voice, memory, performance and the emotional temperature of a scene.

Her style favors softness with force: long hair, luminous skin, movement, warmth, romance and tension. In the studio, she brings instinct, melody and atmosphere.

MOON

Moon is an artist, editor and creative director. Her work begins with construction: the title, the page, the frame, the order of the songs, the reason an object belongs.

Her style is restrained, exacting and deliberate. She is drawn to systems, visual identity, sound, packaging and the architecture of a brand.

THE BEGINNING

Music entered their lives early. Performances, recording, rehearsals and cameras were part of the background before they were part of a plan.

Over time, the work became more intentional. Songs became eras. Images became campaigns. A project became a house.

THEIR CODES

The codes of Sun and Moon are contrast, devotion, restraint, atmosphere and permanence. They return to black and white, silver and gold, skin and shadow, the stage and the private room.

STARGIRLS is built from these oppositions. Not as a concept, but as the natural result of two sisters making work from different instincts.

IN THEIR WORDS

“Creation begins with intention.”
“Beauty is everlasting.”
“Nothing should exist without a reason.”
“The work has to feel like a world.”
