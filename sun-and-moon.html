SUN & MOON

01

The Beginning

Every House begins with a story. Ours began before STARGIRLS had a name. Raised between New York, New Jersey and Florida, we learned early that stability was not something given. It was something built.

Music was always present. As children, we performed before we were old enough to understand the world around us. What remained constant was not where we lived, but each other.

02

Two Sisters

We did not always see the world the same way. Sun moved through feeling. Moon moved through observation.

What once felt like conflict became our greatest strength. Every disagreement taught us another language. Every difference became another perspective.

03

Sun

Sun believes beauty should be felt before it is understood.

She is instinct. Romantic. Unapologetically emotional. Her world begins with feeling and ends with memory.

04

Moon

Moon believes beauty begins with intention.

She observes before she speaks. Questions before she concludes. Every detail exists for a reason.

05

Leaving

There comes a moment when everyone must decide whether to inherit a life or create one.

We chose uncertainty over comfort because certainty belonged to someone else's vision. Every obstacle forced us to become more independent, more resourceful, and more certain of why we began.

06

The House

STARGIRLS was never intended to exist only as music.

It was imagined as a House. A place where music, fragrance, beauty and objects could share the same philosophy.

Sun imagines. Moon questions. One creates emotion. The other creates structure. Neither exists without the other.

07

The Philosophy

Beauty is everlasting.

Luxury is quality.

Success is built, never inherited.

Freedom begins within.

Time does not define creation.
