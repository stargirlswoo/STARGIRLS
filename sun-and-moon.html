SUN & MOON

“Every House begins with a story. Ours began long before it had a name.”

Sun and Moon, New York, 2026

Sun and Moon grew up between New York, New Jersey and Florida. Their childhood was marked by frequent change, music, and the search for stability. They performed together from an early age, first as members of a family band and later through work they pursued independently.

Although raised under the same roof, the sisters rarely experienced the world in the same way. Sun responded instinctively, guided by feeling and imagination. Moon questioned everything, preferring observation before conclusion. What appeared to be opposition would eventually become the foundation of their creative partnership.

STARGIRLS was founded on the belief that creation should not be divided into disciplines. Music, fragrance, beauty, film and objects would each belong to the same universe and speak the same language.

DEVOTED

Music entered their lives before either sister imagined it could become a profession. Childhood performances, rehearsals and recording sessions formed part of daily life. Performing was never approached as spectacle, but as routine.

As they grew older, music remained the constant element through changing homes, schools and cities. What began as childhood participation gradually became deliberate practice.

“We never learned how to separate life from creation.”

DISTINCT

The sisters did not always understand one another. Sun trusted intuition. Moon trusted analysis. One searched for feeling before certainty. The other searched for certainty before feeling.

What once appeared to create disagreement gradually revealed itself as complement. Rather than attempting to think alike, they learned to preserve the difference between them.

That distinction continues to define every decision made within the House.

INDEPENDENT

There came a point when Sun and Moon decided to rely upon their own work rather than wait for opportunity.

They learned production, photography, editing, recording, design and creative direction themselves, investing in their own equipment before expanding their projects. Building slowly became preferable to building quickly.

The House developed through discipline as much as imagination.

OBSERVANT

Moon approaches creation through observation. Ideas are examined before they are accepted. Every detail must justify its existence. Structure is not regarded as limitation, but as clarity.

Her interest extends beyond music into systems, design and the architecture of a brand. She believes coherence gives objects permanence.

INSTINCTIVE

Sun creates through feeling. Emotion precedes explanation. She approaches music, image and performance through instinct rather than calculation.

Memory, intimacy and atmosphere remain recurring themes throughout her work. Her belief that beauty should first be experienced before it is understood continues to influence the identity of the House.

THE HOUSE

STARGIRLS was never conceived solely as a musical project.

From its beginning, it was imagined as a House where music, fragrance, beauty, moving image and objects would be developed according to a shared philosophy.

Rather than existing as separate ventures, each discipline contributes to a single creative language. The House continues to evolve, while remaining faithful to the principles upon which it was founded.
